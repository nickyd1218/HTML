City Latitude vs. Humidity

The following plot shows the City Latitute vs. Humidity for the 500+ samples used in the dataset. There appears to be no strong correlation between the humidity of a location and how close it is to the equator. However, as the equator is approached, it seems to be less likely to experience a humidity under 50%.
